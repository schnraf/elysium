Elysium

A perhaps not so wrong tale about the origins of humankind.

About this story

Elysium is known to the old Greeks as the place where the dead would trespass to. On these elysian plains supposedly gathered and dwelled heroes of ancient times.

Chapter 1

How it all began

Imagine a Planet. A planet like Earth. Plenty of life dwelling on its surface. Animals on land and in the sea. Biurds high up in the sky. Human civilization ongoing. Buildings out of concrete everywhere you look.

The people in these cities progress somewhat further everyday. Becoming efficient in their labour. Innovating in their technology. Expanding their knowledge. No religious fanaticism.

They aim to reach the stars. Already went to the next planet in their solar system. A planet of the same size as their own. These people even colonized a good chunk of it. But they wanted more. Getting deeper into the far wide space. Planting their seed onto new worlds.
